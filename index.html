HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Be the first to empty your hand.

MATCHING: You can play a card if it matches the Rank (Number) or Suit (Color) of the top card on the pile.

STACKING: You can play multiple cards if they have the same Rank (e.g., three 7s) or continue a Suit chain.

STRATEGY: The LAST SELECTED CARD will end up on top. Use this to change the suit!

RESHUFFLE: When the deck is empty, the played pile is shuffled into a new deck, and the top card changes randomly.
